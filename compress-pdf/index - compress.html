FAQ

Which level should I use?

For email or web sharing use Screen or eBook. For readable documents use Balanced. For archiving or printing use High or Print.

Why is my PDF barely smaller?

PDFs without embedded images (text-only or pure vector) compress very little since there are no image pixels to reduce. Try Screen level for maximum effect on image-heavy PDFs.

Does it freeze my browser?

No. The compression runs in a Web Worker (a background thread), so the page stays completely responsive during processing even for large files.

Is my file safe?

100% private. Everything runs in WebAssembly inside your browser. Your PDF never leaves your device at any point.
