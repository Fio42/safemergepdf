FAQ

Are my files uploaded to any server?

No. Your PDF is read locally and converted inside your browser using WebAssembly. Nothing is uploaded.

What DPI should I choose?

72 DPI is fine for screen use. 150 DPI works well for most purposes. 300 DPI is recommended for printing.

Can I convert just one page?

All pages are converted. You can then download only the pages you need.

Is there a page limit?

No hard limit, but large PDFs with many pages may take a moment to process depending on your device.

Can I convert to PNG instead?

Currently only JPG is supported. PNG support is planned for a future update.
