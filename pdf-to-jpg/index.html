FAQ

Is my file uploaded to a server?

No. PDF rendering happens entirely in your browser using PDF.js by Mozilla. Nothing is sent anywhere.

What DPI should I use?

72 DPI for quick web previews. 150 DPI for general use. 300 DPI for printing or sharp zooming.

Can I download all pages at once?

Yes, click "Download all" and every page will be saved as a separate JPG.

Does it work with password-protected PDFs?

Not directly. First remove the password with our Unlock PDF tool, then convert here.

Is there a page limit?

No hard limit. Very large PDFs may take longer depending on your device speed.
