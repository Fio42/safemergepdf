FAQ

What image formats are supported?

JPG, PNG, and WEBP files are all supported.

Can I combine multiple images into one PDF?

Yes. Add as many images as you want. Each image becomes one page. Drag to reorder before converting.

What page size should I choose?

"Fit to image" makes each page exactly the size of the image. A4 and Letter scale images to fit standard paper sizes.

Are my images uploaded anywhere?

No. Everything runs in your browser using WebAssembly (Pyodide + Pillow). Nothing leaves your device.

Is there a limit on number of images?

No hard limit. More images means more processing time.
