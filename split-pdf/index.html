FAQ

What is the difference between the two modes?

All pages splits every page into a separate PDF file. Custom range extracts a specific set of pages into a single PDF.

How do I write a custom range?

Use commas to separate pages and dashes for ranges. Example: 1-3, 5, 7-9 extracts pages 1, 2, 3, 5, 7, 8, and 9.

Are my files uploaded anywhere?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser via WebAssembly. Your files never leave your device.

Is there a page limit?

No page limit. Files can be up to 100 MB.

Can I extract just one page?

Yes. Use custom range mode and enter a single page number, like "3".
